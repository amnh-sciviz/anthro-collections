Exhibition History
• BODY ART: MARKS OF IDENTITY. AMNH, NEW YORK, NY. November 1999 - May 2000
Exhibition Label: Ceremonial jar. Ceramic. A.D. 500-800. This jar, which takes the form of a creature with human arms and the head of a bat, is probably meant to show a shaman changing from human to animal form. Like a human shaman, the creature has body painting. The jar probably held intoxicating beverages for consumption during ceremonies led by a shaman.
