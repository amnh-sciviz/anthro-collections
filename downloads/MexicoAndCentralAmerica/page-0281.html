Country: PANAMA

Material: CLOTH (COTTON)
Dimensions: L:87.5 W:66.5 (in CM)

Acquisition Year: 2002 [GIFT]
Donor: YULE
Keywords: MOLA CLOTH
Category: TEXTILE
Share Object
Add Object to Portfolio
Curatorial Notes: Mola, a reverse appliquéd cotton textile, is usually made for the front and back of a blouse but this unique mola was not made for a blouse but for sale. A Kuna woman made it right after the first moon landing in July 1969 and it shows the Gemini space capsule and astronauts. She could follow this event on her television. It took her about nine months to finish. Her son sold it to Ms. Yule in the spring of 1970 and she donated it to AMNH in 2002.
