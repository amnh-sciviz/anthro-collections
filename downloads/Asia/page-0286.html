Exhibition Label: Horse figurine. China. 206 BC-AD 220. Bronze. This bronze horse was made during the Han dynasty (206 BC-AD 220). During the Han dynasty, the Chinese mounted an expedition to Ferghana, 2,000 miles west of the Chinese capital, Xian, to acquire superior horses.
