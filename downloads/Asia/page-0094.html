Curatorial Notes: Capable of carrying large amounts of goods and people, Water Buffalo were socially and economically important in Thai society through most of the 20th century. Carts drawn by water buffalo were a major mode of transportation in early 20th century Thailand and a cart like this one would have been pulled by two adult buffalo while a driver stood in the center.
