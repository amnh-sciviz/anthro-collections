Curatorial Notes: Made out of calf hide. There is an eagle image on the back of the coat, made of the dark fur. Coats with the eagle images on their backs are the most ancient ones. They dont have any additional hinged decorations. These coats were worn for especially important ceremonial events, during ritual ceremonies and were passed from generation to generation as a family treasure.
