In touting the efficiency of these skis, [collector Waldemar] Borgoras noted that Even skiers running alongside his dogsled could easily keep up without visible strain for miles.” (VanStone, James. “Hunters, Herders, Trappers and Fishermen” in Crossroads of Continents, p.181); AMNH Jesup North Pacific Expedition (1897-1902).
