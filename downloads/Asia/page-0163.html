Curatorial Notes: Bagobo baskets are both utilitarian and beautiful, and like other parts of their raiment, contribute to enhance their sense of style and movement. The front of this basket is beaded to form a pattern of nested diamonds. The back is not decorated with beads, but with a pleasing woven and painted grid pattern. Beading around the basket's strap continues down into trailing tassels which swayed while one walked.
