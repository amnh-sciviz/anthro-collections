Catalog No: 70.1/ 6052
Culture: BAGOBO

Country: PHILIPPINES
Date: Early 20th Century A.D.
Material: BEAD

Donor: BENEDICT, LAURA WATSON
Keywords: COMB
Category: EQUIPMENT
Share Object
Add Object to Portfolio
Curatorial Notes: This Bagobo woman's comb is composed of both indigenous materials (wood and horsehair) and imported materials (glass beads and mirror fragments). This is one of almost 2,500 objects collected by Laura Watson Benedict when she lived and worked amongst the Bagobo between 1906 and 1908.
Exhibition History
• MUSEE DU QUAI BRANLY 2013. AMNH, NEW YORK, NY. January 2012
