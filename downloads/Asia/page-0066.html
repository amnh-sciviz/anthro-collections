Researchers' Remarks
Cotton has been cultivated in China since roughly 200 BCE. This tiny cuddly toy encompasses aspects of Chinese material and symbolic culture. Speaking to the complexity of the Chinese language, where punning is commonplace, the calico “butterfly cat” motif combines the playful cat with the butterfly to represent luck, beauty, and a wish for longevity. --Collected north of Beijing by sinologist Berthold Laufer in March 1,1902 as one of fifty-six toys totaling $600.
