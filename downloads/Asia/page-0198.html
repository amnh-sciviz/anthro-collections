Exhibition Label: Headband. Palm leaves, glass, cotton, vegetable fiber. Kenyah. Early 20th century. Aso-inspired tattoo designs appear on the beaded headband that was traditionally worn by a Kenyah man. The aso motif is rigidly reserved for those of high rank.
