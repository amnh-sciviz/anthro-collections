Exhibition History
THE HORSE AMNH, NEW YORK, NY. May 2008 - January 2009
Exhibition Label: Funnel. Sakha Republic, Russia. c. 1900. Wood, copper alloy, sinew. To celebrate the renewal of life at the beginning of summer, the Sakha share koumiss, a drink made of fermented mare’s milk. Traditionally, a container shaped like a horse hoof is used to funnel milk into a leather bag, where it is churned with a stick and left to sit for many hours until it is ermented. During the festival, guests drink koumiss from carved wooden goblets with hoof-shaped feet.
