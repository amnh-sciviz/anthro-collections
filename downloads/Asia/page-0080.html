Exhibition History
MYTHIC CREATURES: DRAGONS, UNICORNS & MERMAIDS AMNH, NEW YORK, NY. May 2007
Exhibition Label: “DRAGON BONES”. Sichuan Province, China. c. 1900. In traditional Chinese medicine, longgu, or “dragon bones,” are prescribed as a treatment for numerous ailments, from madness to diarrhea and dysentery. These samples, bought by a collector unfamiliar with Chinese medicine, consist of ordinary rock. But most fragments and powders sold in Chinese pharmacies as dragon bone come from fossil remains of extinct mammals, unearthed from China’s renowned fossil beds.
