Curatorial Notes: Chewing the leaves from the betel plant with powdered lime is mildly stimulating, and played a role in Hanuno courtship. Young women weaved baskets, like this one, for holding betel paraphernalia, and young men prepared lime containers on which they inscribed love songs or personal messages (see #70.1/9601). If a young man and woman were interested in one another, their relationship could begin by exchanging these objects and chewing betel together.
