Curatorial Notes: A pottery drinking vessel in the form of a turtle and decorated with typical Shipibo/Conibo geometric design. This vessel would be hung upright on a hook from the cord around the "turtle's" neck. Part of a large collection of more than 3000 pieces from the Peruvian Montaa region made by Harvey Bassler in the 1920s. He donated it to AMNH in 1934.
