Curatorial Notes: This war club is part of the collection donated to AMNH between1869 and 1890 by Mrs. S.S. Haldeman, one of the earliest acquisitions for the South American ethnographic collection. Made of hard, heavy wood with sharpened corners, it has native cotton cord wrapped around the middle to give the warrior a steady grip of the club. It was carried hanging from the wrist or waist. This club is on exhibition in the Hall of South American Peoples.
