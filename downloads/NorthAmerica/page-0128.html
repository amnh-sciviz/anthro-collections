Researchers' Remarks
RIBBONWORK ON A GREAT EXAMPLE OF SCARLET GREY SAVED LIST. CLOTH IS PIECE DYED WHOLE WITH A WHIP STITCHED COTTON TWILL TAPE BOUND LIST OR SELVAGE. WHEN CLOTH IS DIPPED INTO DYE VAT THE EDGES DO NOT TAKE THE DYE, A RESIST LIKE TIE-DYE. WEBBING IS REMOVED LEAVING A SAWTOOTH PATTERN. THE GREY EDGE IS A HALF INCH OF BLACK OR BLUE WARP THREADS THAT DENOTES CLOTH GRADE AND AREA IN ENGLAND OR COMPANY THAT PRODUCED IT. WILLIAM MAXWELL, PRAIRIE MATERIAL STUDIES CURATOR. July 17, 2012.
