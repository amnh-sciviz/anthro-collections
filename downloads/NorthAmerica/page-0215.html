Exhibition History
• BODY ART: MARKS OF IDENTITY. AMNH, NEW YORK, NY. November 1999 - May 2000
Exhibition Label: Lip ornament. Glass, bone/ivory, quartz, nephrite, stone, metal. 19th century. Labrets made of common materials such as bone and ivory and were worn every day, while those worn for special occasions were made of glass or highly valued stones including quartz, agate and slate. In the late 19th century, glass bottle stoppers sometimes were worn as labrets.
