Curatorial Notes: This Tlingit bentwood box depicting a seal is unusual for its complex pattern and the painting of red on black designs. Bill Holm has called it "the final exam" in Northwest Coast art studies. The box was collected by Lt. George Emmons in 1880s. Exhibited in Objects of Bright Pride (Center for Inter-American Relations, et al. 1978-1990) and Robert Davidson (UBC Museum of Anthropology 2004-2005). Published in Emmons 1907, Boas 1951 and Allen Wardwell 1978.
